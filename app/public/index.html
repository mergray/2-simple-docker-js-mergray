A Triple Threat

Meredith Grey is a world-renowned general surgeon, wife to Mcdreamy, and mother of three. She is currently the head of General Surgery at Grey Sloan Memorial Hospital. Her husband, Derek Shepard, was also head of neurosurgery at Grey Sloan Memorial. Meredith and Derek have three children named Zola, Bailey, and Ellis. She manages to be successful and give time to both her family and her career.
